Budget Control

Look at your entire project budget on one screen. Check your actual spending versus your allocation in an instant

  • Real-time status of raised and pending POs

Add documents or excel sheets and leave it for the tools to do the rest

Vendor Management

This brings negotiations, contracts, rate contracts, and supplier history all under one tab.

  • Get a Quick look into a Rate Contracts and convert them to POs
  • Deepen relationship with vendors with clear communication
  • Check supplier rates, rank your vendors, sort on the basis of past performance
     

Finances

Monitor who has been paid and who is due for a payment in one glance

  • Invoicing - Check raised invoices, dues and payments
  • Integrations
  • Set payment deadlines and reminders
     

Bidding

Create, compare, send and store bids in one location

  • Notify vendors via email, text and notifications
  • Set deadlines and we’ll send reminders
  • Accept bids and turn them into POs with a click
     

Approvals On The Go

Enough of delay in getting approvals. With us, you can cut the approval times by days or even weeks.

  • Quick approvals. You can even send and automate reminders
  • Do it from your mobile
  • Customise your approval flows, the way you want!
